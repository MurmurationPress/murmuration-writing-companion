# World Builder polish smoke test

1. Open the Story World navigator.
2. Close the Writing Companion, then select an entity.
3. Confirm the entity Markdown opens in the centre pane and one Writing Companion leaf opens or reveals on the right.
4. Confirm repeated selections reuse that leaf and return focus to the centre editor.
5. Confirm group counts, entity names, status and event dates are visually separated.
6. Confirm dated events appear oldest first; undated events remain visible afterward.
7. Search for a term matching several events and confirm chronological ordering is retained.
8. Confirm the inspector presents identity, summary, scope and sources as readable sections and wrapped values.
9. Check light and dark themes.
10. Confirm manuscript navigation and prose-first Story World authoring remain unchanged.
